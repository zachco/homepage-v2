Hi, I'm Zach Cohen

I'm a user experience designer and researcher with a passion for sustainability, health, and technology for social good. I'm currently pursuing my graduate degree in Human Factors in Information Design at Bentley University. I work as a research and design assistant in the User Experience Center and serve on the board of the User Experience Graduate Association.

Previously, I was a Product Designer at Integrate, an enterprise marketing software company, where I worked to improve the data accessibility and actionability of our platform.

My professional work has been focused on complex, enterprise applications. I enjoy tackling these challenges as I have the opportunity to design beyond the screen, and collaborate with areas of the entire product team, from strategy and research to implementation and continued growth.
