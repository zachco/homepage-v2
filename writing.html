More about my newsletter

Here I’ll be giving any big updates about my work/life, sharing my new writing, posts from my blog, and regular contemplative thoughts about things that pique my interest.

Without the reliance on social media, it’ll be my cozy way of staying in touch with you while I continue to work on my other projects in the background - and once they’re ready for the world you’ll be the first to know!

Keeping it casual like a letter from a friend, I encourage you to reach out to me with any suggestions or feedback. I’m excited to have you along for the ride.
